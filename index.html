A Community

The Stik People NFT collection is a collection of NFTs made from hand-drawn images of The Stik People. The collection features an organized society with qualities such as spatiotemporal cohesion, leadership, structure, division of labor and so on. New members of the society with different community ranks from the supreme leader to the normal member of the society will be uploaded to the NFT Collection gradually.

Organization of the STIK COMMUNITY


The Stik Community/Society is organized with different social and community ranks. The Stik people NFT Collection is made up of 1 Supreme leader, 1 Prime Minister, 1 General, 12 Council-men, 500 Soldiers, and 9,485 other community members to make a 10,000 Unique NFTs collection of the Stik People.
The higher the rank, the rare the NFT.

The Supreme Leader


The ultimate leader of the Stik People and the most powerful being in the Stik Community. Easily noticable from the distinct attributes and traits like a Light Golden Mask, a Golden Crown, Golden sword and a Royal Red background.

The Prime Minister


Right below the Supreme leader. The most powerful being after the Supreme Leader. The only being with a Red mask, a silver sword with a golden handle and a Medium Aqua-Marine background.

The General


The general is in-charge of all the Soldiers, responsible for the security of the Stik People and their leaders. Wears a jungle green mask, carries a silver sword with a black shield and has a blush pink background. There is only one general in the stik community.

The Council


Consists of only 12 members. They help the supreme leader with decision making and are responsible for law making. Each member wears a council-man wig, a violet mask and has a Blue Turquoise background. Some members carry a briefcase and others a Law's scroll or both or none.

The Soldiers


Like all soldiers, these ones are responsible for the safety of the Stik people. All soldiers carry either a sword or two, a sword and a shield, a javelin and a sword or a javelin and a shield and they wear a Gunmetal color mask and have a background of one of the three frog green shades used in making their backgrounds. Only 500 Soldiers are in Supply.

The People


The people of the Stik Community. 9,485 unique beings. Wear a mask which is either Black, Blue Jay, Jade, Venom green or Yellow green. Have many other attributes and traits which are unique from one another.
